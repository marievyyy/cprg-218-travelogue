01 Paris France

Our Paris trip was absolutely amazing! I soaked up all the culture and history. Each day of this trip brought something new for me to discover.

Day 1 Ofcourse we had to start our trip with visiting a café. We head out to get some buttery croissants from Pain Pain and cappuccinos from a quaint little café. After that we walked to Sacre-Coeur and through the Montmartre neighborhood (fun fact: it's where the famous John Wick 4 stairs scene was filmed). Then we had unforgettable lunch at Chez Janou (highly recommended this).   Our afternoon was We started to check things off our list, so our afternoon activity was packed! We started by exploring the Louvre Gardens followed by a tour of the Louvre Museum at 3:30 PM (hello, Mona Lisa!). Afterward, we climbed to the top of the Arc de Triomphe and walked back to the Eiffel Tower area in the 7th Arrondissement. I was a very surreal moment. Steps count: 38,560 steps (I didn't plan on resting for this vacation hehe)

Day 2We dedicated the entire day exploring the grandeur of Versailles, and it is such a memorable experience. The lavish palace and the meticulously maintained gardens were absolutely breathtaking. We had a quick lunch at the on-site café to give us a chance to recharge before continuing our adventure. It was a long day, but absolutely worth it! I definitely would recommend visiting if you plan to visit Paris.

Day 3On our third day, we decided to take it easy. We started our day with a delicious almond croissant and a cup of coffee at Café Richard. After that, we visited the stunning stained-glass windows of Sainte-Chapelle and explored the charming streets of the Latin Quarter. For lunch, we treated ourselves to some ramen at Kodawari Ramen. In the afternoon we visited the Musée d'Orsay and finally got to see some famous art of Claude Monet and Vincent Van Gogh. Then we head out to Palace des Invalides and we wrapped up the day with a lovely dinner at La Potager.
